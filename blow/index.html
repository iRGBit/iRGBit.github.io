How the speech/scream filter works This is intentionally simple: blowing tends to create broadband noisy energy, often with more high-frequency content. Speech and screaming tend to have stronger tonal/voiced patterns. The app compares high-frequency energy against low-frequency energy and only boosts the bar when the signal looks more like noisy breath than voice. It is not perfect, but it is good enough for playful interaction prototypes.
