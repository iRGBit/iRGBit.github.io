Solo Ono

New Zealand-formed and based in Graz, Solo Ono make sharp-edged indie rock with post-punk tension, dark humour, and a raw, live-wire feel.

About

Post-punk tension, indie hooks, hemispheres colliding.

Solo Ono sit somewhere between Aotearoa roots and a Graz home base, pushing garage-pop immediacy into something tighter, rougher, and more restless. The result feels handmade, wiry, and very alive.
